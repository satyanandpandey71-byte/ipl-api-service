API Endpoints

Base URL: http://127.0.0.1:5000

GET
/api/teams
Returns list of all IPL teams
GET
/api/team-record?team=Mumbai Indians
Overall record of a team — wins, losses, titles
GET
/api/teamVteam?team1=MI&team2=CSK
Head to head record between two teams
GET
/api/batting-record?batsman-name=RG Sharma
Batting stats — runs, average, strike rate, 50s, 100s
GET
/api/bowling-record?bowler-name=JJ Bumrah
Bowling stats — wickets, economy, average, best figure
